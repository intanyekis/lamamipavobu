KJ Apa Bio (Wiki)

Who is KJ Apa?

KJ Apa is a Kiwi TV and a film actor. Multi-talented KJ also plays guitar.

He came to recognition in the entertainment industry after featuring in soap opera in New Zealand named “Shortland Street”.

Presently, he is famous for starring in the American teenage drama series, “Riverdale”.

Additionally, he is also known for starring in 2017 in the comedy-drama film, “A Dog’s Purpose”.

He portrayed the character of Ethan Montgomery in the film.

KJ Apa’s early life, Childhood, and Education

KJ was born in the capital city of New Zealand, Auckland. His birth name is Keneti James Fitzgerald “KJ” Apa.

He was born on June 17, 1997. KJ is of Scottish, English, and Samoan ethnic background.

The name of his parents is Keneti Apa(father) and Tessa Callander(mother). His father, Keneti is the chief of the village, Samoa.

He played guitar and piano at a very young age. He calls his mother Tess and says that she is his best friend.

As per his education, he attended high school at King’s College, Auckland.

KJ Apa’s Career, Salary, Net Worth

KJ is active in his acting career since 2013. He started his acting career in his home country, New Zealand.

In 2013, KJ Apa joined the cast of Soap Opera of New Zealand, “Shortland Street”. He portrayed the role of Kate Jenkins in the show.

He featured on that show from 2013 to 2015, starring in a total of 46 episodes.

Afterward, he appeared in another TV series named “The Cul De Sac” playing the role of Jack.

These minor roles helped him to gain a little recognition in the entertainment industry.

Then KJ Apa moved to the United States. Following his small roles, he gained massive success after starring in the hit American teenage series, “Riverdale”.

He plays the lead role of Archie Andrews in the show. Similarly, he also featured in the comedy-drama film, “A Dog’s Purpose”.

Presently, he is waiting for his film, “Altar Rock” to release. Altar Rock is a thriller that is inspired by the Boston Marathon bombing of 2013.
