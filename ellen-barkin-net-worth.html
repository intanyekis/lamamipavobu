Ellen Barkin Net Worth

Ellen Barkin is an American actress. Ellen became one of the most famous figures in Hollywood after breaking into the public with a role in the 1982 picture “Diner.” Her ‘tough-cookie demeanour’ is well-known. In the late 90s, she earned an Emmy for her performance in the made-for-television film “Before Women Had Wings

Barkin’s squinting eyes and somewhat off-kilter facial features distinguish her apart from the stereotypical Hollywood female. Her unique features helped her gain a key role in “Sea of Love” starring Al Pacino.

She received a Golden Globe nomination for her role in 1991’s “Switch,” and she went on to star in several famous films during the 1990s. Her cinematic career lasted far into the 2000s and 2010, and she made her Broadway debut in 2011.

Barkin is also well-known for her work on television in shows like “Animal Kingdom.” Ellen is an accomplished producer with a variety of credentials outside of acting.

How much is Ellen Barkin worth?

The actress has a net worth of $80 million. She accumulated her worth through her acting career. She also received $40 million in her divorce settlement from Ronald Perelman.
