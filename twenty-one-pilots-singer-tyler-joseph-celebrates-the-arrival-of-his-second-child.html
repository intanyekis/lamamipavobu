Twenty One Pilots singer Tyler Joseph celebrates the arrival of his second child

Twenty One Pilots singer Tyler Joseph celebrates the arrival of his second child as he jokes he had ‘a great time’ during the delivery

By Alesia Stanford For Dailymail.Com

Published: 16:22 EDT, 12 April 2022 | Updated: 17:31 EDT, 12 April 2022

 Tyler Joseph is now the proud papa of two daughters.

The Twenty One Pilots singer and his wife, Jenna, welcomed their second daughter into their growing family.

The baby was born on Friday, April 8, and the Tear in my Heart singer made the announcement over the weekend on Twitter sharing a photo of mom, baby and dad from their hospital room. 

Proud papa:  Tyler Joseph and his wife, Jenna, are now the proud parents of two little girls. The newest arrival joined the family on April 8.  In posting this delivery room photo, the Twenty One Pilots singer joked he ‘had a great time’

‘New baby arrived yesterday. I had a great time,’ he wrote. ‘Already named her, but still open for suggestions.’

The couple recently celebrated their seventh wedding anniversary. They are parents to two-year-old Rosie Robert. 

The family hasn’t shared the new little one’s name yet.

Good times: The Tear in my Heart singer and his wife have been married for seven years

Tyler tweeted he was ‘letting jenna tell everyone in her instagram post that i’m sure she’ll get around to in a couple months.’

The 33-year-old frontman was much more forthcoming when he excitedly announced to the world baby number two was on the way at the 2021 MTV Video Music Awards. 

‘My wife is pregnant! I didn’t want to text everybody, so I thought I’d let you know right now,’ said Joseph from the stage. ‘Now you know!’

Growing family:  The couple were already parents to two-year-old Rosie, seen here from a photo posted celebrating the girl’s second birthday.  The news they were expecting a second child was not released until Jenna was well into her pregnancy

It wasn’t until after she was well into her pregnancy that Jenna revealed on her Instagram that she and Joseph would be having another girl writing ‘tyler+jenna+rosie+baby sister coming in april (sic.)’ 

For Rosie’s second birthday, the family posted a series of adorable black and white photos in early March.

The charming pictures show the photogenic trio goofing around as they laugh and smile. 

More fun ahead: Oldest daughter Rosie celebrated her second birthday recently and her proud parents marked the occasion by posting a sweet series of photos. The new baby has a name, but the Stressed Out singer said he was ‘letting Jenna tell everyone in her Instagram post that I’m sure she’ll get around to in a couple months’
