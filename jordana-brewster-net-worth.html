Jordana Brewster was born on April 26, 1980, in Panama City, Panama. She has a net worth of $2 million. This Brazilian-American actress and came from Brazil to America when she was nine before joining high school. She attended University of Yale where she studied a degree in English arts and found her passion for acting.

While on campus, she started acting before featuring in numerous films namely As the World Turns, All My Children and The Faculty. These made her famous in the limelight of world entertainment. She won the Teen Choice Award for Choice Movie Actress: Action in the year 2007 in addition to nominations in other categories.

She has also appeared in several films such as The Texas Chainsaw Massacre: The Beginning, Nearing Grace, The Fast and the Furious, The Faculty and many more films. She got married to Andrew Form in the year 2007 and together they are living in California.
