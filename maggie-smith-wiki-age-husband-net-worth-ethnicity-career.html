Maggie Smith Biography

Dame Margaret Natalie Smith, better known as Maggie Smith, was born on December 28, 1934, in Ilford, Essex, England. Nathaniel Smith, a pathologist, and Margaret Hutton, a secretary, raised Smith. Smith’s family relocated to Oxford when she was four years old owing to her father’s profession. Alistair Smith and Ian Smith are her two siblings. Maggie is of mixed (English-Scottish) origin and has British citizenship. Her zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Smith attended Oxford High School and graduated at the age of 16. She then went on to study acting at Oxford Playhouse, where she graduated in 1953.
