Rihanna And Diddy's Son Compete For The Sturdiest Milly Rock

Rihanna showcased her moves all weekend long for Coachella’s launch (April 15). Although she never hit the main stage, all eyes rested on the Bajan artist while she danced the night away in the crowd. Now, another video of the 29-year-old superstar has surfaced that has viewers praying for the day they get to battle the “Work” singer on the dance floor.

As noted by Blavity, Rih engaged in a Milly Rock standoff with Diddy’s youngest son, Christian Combs. The occurrence was made possible by the sounds of Future‘s now platinum single, “Mask Off.” The aforementioned track sparked a #MaskOffChallenge, which showcased several musicians’ talents when it comes to mimicking the instrumental.

This isn’t the first time Rih decides to display her moves offstage. She’s showcased her twerking skills to T-Pain’s “Up Down.”

She also showed love to Bobby Shmurda by hitting her two-step to “Hot N*gga.”
