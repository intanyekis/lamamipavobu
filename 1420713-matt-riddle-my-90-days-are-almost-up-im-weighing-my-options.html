Matt Riddle: My 90 Days Are Almost Up, Im Weighing My Options

Matt Riddle contemplates his next move.

Riddle was released by WWE on September 22. Since then, fans have waited to see what his next move will be. He posted the following on Twitter as a possible tease of what he’ll do when his non-compete clause is up:

My 90 days are almost up and I’m weighing my options ? #onlyfans #bro #dude #stallion #wap #wab #abs #speedo #thirstythursday #thirsty #fansly

Matt Riddle is a former WWE United States Champion and a two-time WWE Raw Tag Team Champion. Riddle previously teased a new project involving “Riddle OG” and recently announced he and his girlfriend, Misha Montana, are expecting a baby.

Riddle already has at least one match lined up, as he will compete against Rob Van Dam for Big Time Wrestling. The promotion’s “The Reunion 3: Cavalcade of Legends” is scheduled to take place on March 9, 2024, in Chillicothe, Ohio, at the OU-Shoemaker Center.

In addition to Riddle and RVD, the following names will appear:

  • Kurt Angle
  • Rob Van Dam
  • Matt Riddle
  • The Rock n Roll Express
  • Tommy Rich
  • Tugboat
  • Ernest Miller
  • Kevin Sullivan
  • Missy Hyatt
  • Baby Doll
  • Glacier
  • Judais
