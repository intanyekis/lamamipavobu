Rita Lynn Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Rita Lynn was born on December 2, 1921 in New Orleans, Louisiana, USA as Frances Rita Piazza. She was an actress, known for Code of the West (1947), Cast a Long Shadow (1959) and Madigan (1968). She was married to Frank Maxwell, Arthur William Debernarde and William M. Lynn. She died on January 21, 1996 in Santa Monica, California, USA. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
