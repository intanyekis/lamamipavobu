Dana White gifted brand-new BMW days after 400,000 present from f***ing lunatics at casino

UFC CEO Dana White had a better weekend than most people.

After years of working to make the UFC a mainstream sports promotion, White is believed to have amassed a $500million (£400m) fortune, but he doesn’t need to spend any of it because his new friends are extremely generous.

The MMA mogul recently revealed online influencer Razzaqlocks gifted him a brand new 2024 BMW 7 Series Sedan – a luxury car worth around $97,000.

“There’s a kid named Razz. You see him doing a lot of prize-picks stuff.

“Shows up here. He buys me a 2024 BMW 760 XI. And just to give you an idea of what this car does, all you gotta do is push a button, and then the doors open.
