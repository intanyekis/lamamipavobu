Guy Ritchie's 2019 action-comedy film, The Gentlemen, became an instant cult classic, grossing over $115 million worldwide on a $22 million budget.

The film left a lasting impression on audiences with its stylish visuals, witty dialogue, and stellar cast. The excitement continues as Ritchie brings his signature style to a television spin-off of the same name.

The show is set in the same universe as the original 2019 movie, but centers around a new set of characters played by talented cast members.

Although Netflix's The Gentlemen shares narrative and thematic similarities with its parent film, it follows a standalone narrative that spans eight episodes.

The Gentlemen series promises to delve deeper into the criminal underworld explored in the movie. Theo James, known for his roles in the Divergent series and Sanditon, takes on the lead role.

Joining him is a talented ensemble cast featuring familiar faces from Hollywood, including Vinnie Jones, Kaya Scodelario, Giancarlo Esposito, Daniel Ings, Joely Richardson, and Peter Serafinowicz.
