Here's What Miley Cyrus Has Done Since Divorcing Liam Hemsworth

In August 2019, Miley and Liam broke up. Miley Cyrus (who later got dissed by Chris Hemsworth) and Liam Hemsworth (who's Chris Hemsworth's brother) announced their divorce, just eight months after tying the knot in a small wedding ceremony in Tennessee. Fans were completely blindsided by the split. Since they had just tied the knot, many were under the impression that these two were going to spend the rest of their lives together, particularly since they had already shared an eight-year, on-off relationship.

After their split, there was gossip that Miley was wasting no time moving on from The Hunger Games star. In fact, she was spotted making out with Brody Jenner's ex-wife, Kaitlynn Carter. Sources claimed that Miley and Kaitlynn were seeing one another, but their relationship didn't last long. Cyrus would soon fall head over heels in love with the blonde Aussie hunk, Cody Simpson, before announcing her plans to drop a seventh studio album sometime in 2020.

What else has Miley been up to since calling it quits Liam Hemsworth? Here's what you need to know.

13 She Signed A New Management Deal

In February 2020, it was revealed that Miley Cyrus had parted ways with her longtime management company, CAA, to sign with William Morris Endeavor Entertainment instead. The timing seems perfect since Miley is finalizing her seventh studio album. Her new management team will be there to help when she drops the new record.

12 She Dated Brody Jenner’s Ex Kaitlynn Carter

Following her split with Liam during the summer of 2019, Miley wasted no time starting a new relationship - this time, it was with a woman named Kaitlynn Carter, whom she was seen kissing while on vacation in Italy. Carter had just ended her marriage with Brody Jenner, so she could relate to what Miley was going through. Their relationship was in full swing for a couple of weeks before they decided to call it quits.

11 She Released A Song About Her Marriage Downfall

After calling it quits with Liam, Miley went on to release an emotional ballad titled, "Slide Away". In the song's lyrics, she talks about a relationship that has run its course. While she never revealed whether the track was about her marriage, it seemed pretty obvious, given all of the clues in the song, along with the fact that she released it in the wake of their split.

10 She Started Dating Another Aussie

Miley Cyrus clearly wasn’t wasting any time after her relationship with Liam ended - just weeks after her romance with Kaitlynn had reportedly ended, she was seen getting cozy with Australian singer, Cody Simpson. By October 2019, the couple confirmed they were seeing one another, and they’ve been together ever since.

9 She Shaved Cody’s Head During The COVID-19 Pandemic

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Miley and Cody Simpson have been isolating together. In April 2020, the former Hannah Montana star shocked fans when she was seeing shaving her boyfriend’s head in an Instagram video posted on Simpson’s page. Apparently, he’d been wanting to sport a new look for some time, and since getting a professional haircut amid the global pandemic was impossible, Miley played hairdresser.

8 She Collaborated With Ariana Grande And Lana Del Rey

In September 2019, Miley joined forces with Ariana Grande and Lana Del Rey for the ultimate collaboration. They collaborated on the song, ‘Don’t Call Me Angel.’ The track was lifted from the poorly-received Charlie’s Angel’s reboot, and though the film bombed at the box office, fans were obsessed with ‘DCMA’ and its visually appealing music video. The song went on to peak at No. 13 on the Billboard Hot 100.

7 She Dispelled Engagement Rumors With Cody

Just weeks after going public with Cody Simpson, Miley was seen sporting what appeared to be an engagement ring, leaving fans wondering whether she was honestly looking to tie the knot again - especially since it hadn’t even been more than six months since she announced her divorce from Liam. However, it turned out that the ring Miley was sporting on her wedding finger was actually just a piece of jewelry owned by Cody. She liked it enough to wear it on a day out. Phew.

6 They Had Thanksgiving Dinner At Her Family’s House

In November 2019, Miley’s younger sister, Noah Cyrus, posted a photo of herself having Thanksgiving dinner with her family - and interestingly enough, Cody Simpson also happened to be there. Just a month before their get-together, Simpson and Cyrus announced their relationship, so it seemed fairly soon for the pop star to be introduced to his girlfriend’s parents.

By the looks of things, the Cyrus family absolutely loves the 23-year-old and certainly approves of his relationship with Miley.

5 She Launched Her Own Online Talk Show

Amid the Coronavirus health crisis, Miley announced in March 2020 that she was going to launch her very own online talk show, with celebrity guests, interviews, and Q&A sessions. She wanted to do her part to encourage people to stay at home. The show, which had been airing Monday to Friday, featured famous people, including Elton John, Hailey Bieber, Selena Gomez, Dua Lipa, and Reese Witherspoon.

4 She Sold Her House Near Liam’s Property

In February 2020, it was revealed that Miley had sold her $2.5 million, fire-damaged Malibu home, which is opposite Liam’s pad. She purchased the mansion in 2016, but following her divorce from Liam, she sold the property for measly $1.7 million., according to MSN. 3 She Helped Promote Her Dad’s ‘Old Town Road’ Song

When Lil Nas X released his single, "Old Town Road", featuring Billy Ray Cyrus, Miley supported the track by plastering its artwork all over her social media. Her helping hand was one reason why the track hit the top spot on the Billboard Hot 100. "Old Town Road" was the best-selling song of 2019 and the longest-running No.1 single in Billboard’s history.

2 She Donated $10 Million For COVID-19 Relief

In April 2020, Miley Cyrus announced, in partnership with MAC Cosmetics, that she was going to be donating $10 million to assist COVID-19 relief efforts, according to Daily Mail. Her generous deed, alongside the cosmetics firm she has worked with in the past, will help provide a lot of important services. Money donated will assist with treatment of vulnerable patients, and food delivery.

1 She Started Work On Her Seventh Album

Miley Cyrus has made it no secret that she’s been working on her seventh album. Work on this record began in 2017. It’s been a work in progress for the singer, who has already released two songs from the record - "Mother's Daughter" and "Slide Away" - but fans are still waiting for the former Disney star to confirm a release date for the highly-anticipated project. It appears to be delayed by the COVID-19 pandemic.

The album, which features production work from Mark Ronson and longtime collaborator, Mike Will Made-It, is expected to hit stores later this year.
