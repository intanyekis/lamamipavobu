Karreuche Tran Admits To Not Having Any Issues With Rihanna

Karrueche Tran doesn’t have any ill feelings. Despite being the focal point of yet another one of Chris Brown’s antics (See: Tyson Beckford beef), Tran remains unbothered. The 27-year-old model recently made a pit stop at Big Boy’s show on iHeartRadio’s 92.3 where she kept her cool every time her ex-beau was mentioned.

She simply said she avoids bumping into him at all costs. “I make it so that we don’t have to cross paths so it’s not awkward,” she said, revealing that she actually found out about Brown’s daughter through TMZ, like the rest of us did.

And when it comes to her feelings on Rihanna? She professed there is no bad blood between them, they just happened to date the same guy.

“She is Rihanna, she is a pop star, she’s beautiful, she makes great music,” she continued. “And I’m just me and I’m growing and working into myself, so you can’t really compare us. We’re two completely different girls.”

Keep it classy, Karreuche.
