Erik Karlsson is a Swedish professional ice hockey player who has an estimated net worth of $16.5 million.

Erik Karlsson started his career in 2008 when he played for the Elitserien's Frolunda HC. The team eventually won the Anton Cup when they clinched the Swedish Junior Ice Hockey Championship title against Brynas IF.  He was ranked fourth among European hockey players by the NHL Central Scouting Service.

On the same year, the Ottawa Senators chose him as the 15th overall during the NHL Draft. Then in 2009, he joined the roster to play for the team. Karlsson also played during the NHL All-Star Game in 2011.

Karlsson signed a seven-year contract extension in 2012. Two years later, he was the 9th captain for the Senators.

Erik Sven Gunnar Karlsson was born on May 31, 1990 in Landsbro, Sweden. In 2012, he is married to Therese Karlsson.
